### Creating a Task
1. Click the **"New Task"** button on the board.
2. Fill in the title, description, and initial status (usually "To Do").
3. Assign priority labels: **Urgent**, **Normal**, or **Low**.
4. Click **Save**.

### Moving a Task
- **Drag and Drop**: Simply drag a task card from one column (e.g., "To Do") to another (e.g., "In Progress").
- **Status Update**: Click on a task to open details and change the status from the dropdown menu.

### Assigning a Task
1. Open a task card.
2. support selecting a team member from the **Assignee** dropdown.
3. *Note: Tasks initiated by the Captain have priority.*

## ğŸš¦ Workflow Rules

1. **Prioritization**: Tasks at the top of the list are higher priority.
2. **Review Process**:
   - Do **NOT** move tasks directly to "Done".
   - Move completed tasks to **"Review"**.
   - The Captain must review and approve before moving to "Done".
3. **Communication**:
   - Use the **Comments** section in each task for specific updates.
   - Use Telegram for general announcements.
